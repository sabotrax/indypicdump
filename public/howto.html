How-to indypicdump

  • · We'll create a random username for you. You may like it or not.
  • · Your pictures won't show up immediately. Allow up to 30 minutes for processing.
  • · Pictures are displayed randomly. Just wait 15 seconds.
  • · Send one picture only per mail.
  • · Keep pictures larger than 400 pixels on the longest side.
  • · Usernames are bound to email addresses. We'll create a new username for every address you send from. See "Adding email addresses to usernames" below.

User Management

  • Adding email addresses to usernames

  • · You have, for example, been sending pictures from speedy@gonzales.com as "yellow sombrero" but want to post from nacho@doctorcheese.com as the same user.
  • · Send an email from nacho@doctorcheese.com to me@indypicdump.com with the subject "i am yellow sombrero".
  • · You'll receive a confirmation email to speedy@gonzales.com.

  • Accepting and declining messages

  • · There is a messaging system so you can have feedback on your pictures. It's turned off by default but in turning it on visitors of the website can rant at you or express their respect and admiration.
  • · To accept messages send an email to me@indypicdump.com with the subject "accept messages".
  • · You'll receive a confirmation email to complete the process.
  • · Use "decline messages" accordingly.

  • Inviting users to dumps (also inviting new users)

  • · If you are a member of a dump you can invite others to it (you'll be automatically if you created it).
  • · Send an email to me@indypicdump.com with the subject "open name-of-dump for someone@somehost.com".
  • · After your confirmation and if the person you invited is already using indypicdump she can start adding pictures to the dump outright.
  • · New users but will get an invitation.
